Predicting Citation Counts of Papers in the Biomedical Literature

In this work, we presented methods to predict the citation count of a given publication before it is published. Unlike other research, we only need its textual information and its references to effectively decide whether it is going to be good publication. Additionally, there were other work that tries to predict the citation counts in various domains however they did not incorporate domain specific data for which we argued to have some important inclinations. Therefore, we also use biological data to enhance the prediction.
